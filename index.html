01

The works

Seven systems. One rule each. No two moments alike — and every one reproducible from a seed.

  1. I

    Currents

    Ten thousand specks adrift on an invisible tide of noise.

  2. II

    Murmuration

    Three rules per bird, and a flock that no bird intends.

  3. III

    Coral

    Two chemicals, feeding and fleeing, painting living Turing patterns.

  4. IV

    Heartwood

    One branch becomes two; a tree unfurls and sways in a noise wind.

  5. V

    Mycelium

    A slime mould that wires itself into the most efficient map.

  6. VI

    Ink

    A true fluid you push with a fingertip; it keeps drifting after.

  7. VII

    Cosmos

    A slow fall through stars, nebula and the occasional meteor.
